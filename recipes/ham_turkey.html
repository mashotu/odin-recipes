Ham & Turkey Sandwich

Description

A simple ham & turkey sandwich that can be made home with simple ingredients for one person

Home

Ingredients

  • 1 round bread loaf
  • 2 tablspoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons yellow mustard
  • 2 cups shredded iceberg lettuce
  • 1/2 pound sliced deli ham
  • 1/2 pound sliced deli turkey
  • 6 slices swiss cheese
  • 6 slices cheddar or colby jack cheese
